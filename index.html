Fixing Conversion Leakage in a ₹1.5L Data Analytics Funnel

Redesigned the enrollment journey for non-technical professionals by replacing a generic funnel with a domain-personalized decision system.

Executive Summary

The Coding Ninjas Data Analytics Bootcamp was facing significant drop-offs across the funnel. High-intent professionals engaged but failed to convert.

The root cause was a mismatch between user intent and product communication.

  • Designed for freshers
  • No segmentation
  • Static funnel
