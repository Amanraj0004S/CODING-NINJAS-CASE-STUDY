01 — The Problem

High-intent professionals were dropping off. Silently. Expensively.

A 32-year-old HR Manager clicks a high-intent ad. Watches a full 45-minute webinar. Reads the landing page carefully. And leaves — without booking a consultation, without paying the ₹5,000 reservation fee.

She was not uninterested. She was not price-sensitive. She was invisible to the product.

5–12yr
Average experience of dropping users. Senior professionals with real purchasing power and genuine motivation.
₹5K
Reservation fee never paid. Every drop-off is immediate, measurable, and entirely preventable revenue loss.
₹1Cr/mo
Estimated monthly revenue leakage from non-technical professional drop-off at the landing page.
02 — The Insight

Users are not dropping due to lack of interest.
They are dropping due to identity mismatch.

The funnel was built for a 21-year-old CS student. It was speaking the wrong language to the wrong person — at the highest-stakes moment in the entire customer journey.

04 — PM Thinking

Replace a static brochure with an identity-driven system

The solution is not a new product. It is a new conversation — one that recognises who the user is before it tries to sell them anything.

01
The Hook
Remove jargon. Add identity.
Replace Python, SQL, Jupyter from the hero. Surface a binary choice: Student vs Working Professional. Zero commitment. Maximum signal.
02
The Intent Fork
Supercharge vs Switch — two different products.
Upskilling within a domain and career switching are fundamentally different journeys. Treat them as such — with a UX nudge that reframes existing experience as an asset.
03
Personalisation Engine
Domain selection — the product knows you.
A 2×4 interactive domain grid. The user self-selects. The product now knows exactly who it is speaking to and can reflect their reality back at them.
04
The Kill Shot
Dynamic lead capture — you see yourself in the outcome.
"Become a Strategic HR Analytics Lead. CTC: ₹15LPA+" — not a generic "Data Analyst." The user sees a future that does not erase their past. Trust is built. Checkout begins.
05 — Engineering Awareness

This can be built and shipped.

The solution is not a design concept. It is a product spec with clear technical requirements and integration points.

SPA Logic
Progressive reveal with state memory
Each step only surfaces after the previous selection. Choices are stored in session state and carried through the entire funnel — no repeated questions, no broken experience.
Dynamic Copy
Lead capture form updates in real time
Headline, salary benchmark, and CTA text are dynamically injected based on selected domain — no page reload. A lookup table maps domains to personalised copy variants.
Video Stitch
10-minute domain intro pre-pended server-side
Upon registration, the backend stitches the appropriate pre-recorded domain intro to the front of the live bootcamp. User receives one seamless link — not two videos.
CRM Tagging
Intent data passed to inside sales
Domain, intent (supercharge vs switch), and funnel path are passed as CRM fields. Sales can personalise every call opening — reducing hang-ups, increasing talk-time duration.
06 — Business Impact

Every metric tied to revenue. No vanity numbers.

↑
Lead Form Completion Rate
Domain-specific copy reduces imposter syndrome at first contact. More non-tech professionals complete the form — they see themselves as the right fit.
↑
10-Minute Webinar Retention
The personalised intro video hooks the user before generic content begins. Retention past 10 minutes proves the domain-specific bridge is working.
↑
Checkout Completion Rate
The North Star. Total volume of non-technical professionals completing the ₹5,000 booking and EMI checkout. Every upstream metric exists to move this one number.
